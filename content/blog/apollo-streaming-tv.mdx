## What Is Apollo Streaming TV?

Apollo TV is a premium IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) service. Unlike Netflix or Disney+, which focus only on on-demand content, Apollo TV combines:

- **Live TV** — News, sports, entertainment, kids, and international channels
- **VOD (Video on Demand)** — Movies and TV series available anytime
- **Catch-up TV** — Watch content you missed from the past few days
- **EPG (Electronic Program Guide)** — See what's on now and what's coming next

All of this is delivered over your internet connection, meaning you can stream on any device, from anywhere in the world.

## How Does Apollo Streaming TV Work?

The process is simple:

1. **Subscribe** — Choose a plan and receive your login credentials via email
2. **Download an app** — Install IPTV Smarters Pro or TiviMate on your device
3. **Log in** — Enter your username, password, and portal URL
4. **Watch** — Browse 40,000+ channels and 100,000+ on-demand titles instantly

The entire setup process takes less than 10 minutes. For step-by-step instructions, visit our [full setup guide](/setup).

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Apollo streaming TV different from Netflix?**
Yes. Netflix is a subscription video-on-demand service with no live TV. Apollo TV includes both live TV (40,000+ channels) and on-demand content (100,000+ titles), plus live sports.

**Do I need special hardware for Apollo streaming TV?**
No special hardware is needed. You can use any device you already own — a Smart TV, phone, Firestick, tablet, or PC. An IPTV player app is all you need.

**How reliable is Apollo streaming TV?**
Apollo TV guarantees 99.9% uptime and uses anti-freeze technology to prevent buffering. It runs on high-performance servers with redundancy built in.

**Can I watch Apollo streaming TV on multiple screens?**
Yes. Depending on your plan, you can stream on 1 to 4 devices at the same time. The 12-month plan supports up to 4 simultaneous streams.

**Does Apollo streaming TV work outside my country?**
Yes. Apollo TV works globally. You can stream from any country as long as you have an internet connection. This is one of its biggest advantages over cable and satellite.

**How fast does my internet need to be?**
Minimum 10 Mbps for HD, 15–20 Mbps for Full HD, and 25–30 Mbps for 4K streaming.

**Can I try Apollo streaming TV for free?**
Yes. Apollo TV offers a free trial on request. Contact the team via WhatsApp to get your test account set up immediately.
