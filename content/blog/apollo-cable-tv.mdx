## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Apollo TV the same as cable TV?**
No. Apollo TV is an IPTV service that delivers content over the internet. Cable TV uses physical coaxial cables. Apollo TV offers more channels at a much lower price with more flexibility.

**Can Apollo TV replace my cable subscription completely?**
For most users, yes. Apollo TV covers live TV, sports, movies, series, and international channels — everything most cable subscribers watch. The only exception is very local channels in some regions.

**Is Apollo TV IPTV legal?**
IPTV technology is legal. Apollo TV provides access to content through its service. We recommend checking the regulations in your country.

**Do I need a new TV to use Apollo TV?**
No. Apollo TV works on any device you already own — including your existing Smart TV, Firestick, phone, or PC.

**What happens if my internet goes down?**
Streaming will pause. Unlike cable, which doesn't require internet, IPTV needs a connection. A stable broadband connection of 10 Mbps or more is recommended.

**Can I keep my local channels with Apollo TV?**
Apollo TV includes local channels from many countries and regions. Contact support to confirm your specific local channels are covered.

**What is the cheapest Apollo TV plan?**
The 12-month plan works out to just $8.25/month — the best value. The 1-month plan is $18/month for those who want to try without commitment.
