## What App Does Apollo IPTV Use?

Apollo TV doesn't have a single branded app — instead, it works with several high-quality third-party IPTV player apps. You enter your Apollo TV credentials into the app, and it connects to the service.

The recommended app is **IPTV Smarters Pro** — it's free, works on every platform, and is the most beginner-friendly option. Other excellent choices include TiviMate (Android), GSE Smart IPTV (iOS), and SS IPTV (Samsung/LG TVs).

## Install Apollo IPTV App on Firestick

The Amazon Firestick is the most popular device for Apollo IPTV. Here's how to set it up:

### Step 1 — Enable Apps from Unknown Sources
Go to **Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options** and turn on **Apps from Unknown Sources** and **ADB Debugging**.

### Step 2 — Install Downloader App
Go to the **Amazon App Store**, search for **Downloader**, and install it. This lets you install apps that aren't in the Amazon store.

### Step 3 — Download IPTV Smarters Pro
Open the Downloader app and enter this URL:
`https://www.iptvsmarters.com`

Navigate to the download section and install IPTV Smarters Pro for Fire TV.

### Step 4 — Enter Your Apollo TV Credentials
Open IPTV Smarters Pro and select **Login with Xtream Codes API**. Enter:
- **Username** — from your Apollo TV welcome email
- **Password** — from your Apollo TV welcome email
- **URL** — your Apollo TV portal URL

Tap **Add User** and wait for your channels to load.

### Step 5 — Start Watching
Your full 40,000+ channel lineup will appear. Browse by category or use the search to find what you want.

## Install on Android Phone or TV Box

Installing the Apollo IPTV app on Android is straightforward:

1. Open the **Google Play Store**
2. Search for **IPTV Smarters Pro** or **TiviMate**
3. Install the app
4. Open the app and select **Xtream Codes API** login
5. Enter your Apollo TV username, password, and portal URL
6. Tap **Add** — channels load within seconds

**TiviMate** is an excellent alternative for Android. It has a cleaner interface and better sports EPG. The free version works well, and the premium version ($5/year) adds recording and multi-screen features.

---

## Install on iPhone or iPad

Apple devices use a different process since iOS doesn't allow all IPTV apps in the App Store. Here's the easiest way:

1. Open the **App Store**
2. Search for **GSE Smart IPTV** or **IPTV Smarters**
3. Install the app (both are free)
4. Open the app and go to **Remote Playlists** or **Xtream Codes**
5. Enter your Apollo TV credentials
6. Tap **Add** and wait for channels to load

**GSE Smart IPTV** is the top choice for iPhone and iPad — it's free, stable, and handles large channel lists well.

## Install on Samsung or LG Smart TV

Most modern Samsung and LG TVs support IPTV apps natively through their app stores.

### Samsung (Tizen OS)
1. Open the **Samsung Smart Hub**
2. Go to **Apps** and search for **IPTV Smarters** or **Smart IPTV**
3. Install and open the app
4. Enter your Apollo TV credentials via Xtream Codes API

### LG (WebOS)
1. Open the **LG Content Store**
2. Search for **SS IPTV** or **IPTV Smarters**
3. Install and open the app
4. Go to **Settings → Playlists** and add your Apollo TV M3U URL

Both Samsung and LG TVs display Apollo TV channels in stunning 4K quality when supported by your TV model.

## Install on Windows PC or Mac

Watching Apollo TV on your computer is simple:

### Option 1 — IPTV Smarters (Recommended)
Download IPTV Smarters for Windows from the Microsoft Store. Log in with your Apollo TV credentials exactly like on other devices.

### Option 2 — VLC Media Player
1. Download **VLC** from videolan.org (free and trusted)
2. Open VLC and go to **Media → Open Network Stream**
3. Enter your Apollo TV M3U playlist URL
4. Click **Play** — all channels load in the playlist

VLC is completely free and available on both Windows and Mac.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Channels Not Loading
- Double-check your username, password, and portal URL for typos
- Make sure your subscription is active
- Contact Apollo TV support on WhatsApp for instant help

### Buffering or Freezing
- Switch from Wi-Fi to a wired Ethernet connection
- Lower the stream quality from 4K to Full HD temporarily
- Restart your router and device

### App Crashing
- Clear the app cache in your device settings
- Uninstall and reinstall the app
- Make sure your device firmware is up to date

### EPG Not Loading
- Wait a few minutes — EPG data can take time to populate
- Go to app settings and manually refresh the EPG
- Check that your portal URL is entered correctly
