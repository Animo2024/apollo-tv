## Apollo TV on Android TV / Google TV

If your Smart TV runs Android TV or Google TV (Sony, Hisense, TCL, Philips, Nvidia Shield), installation is the easiest:

1. Open the **Google Play Store** on your TV
2. Search for **IPTV Smarters Pro**
3. Install and open the app
4. Select **Login with Xtream Codes API**
5. Enter your Apollo TV credentials (username, password, portal URL)
6. Tap **Add User** and wait for channels to load
7. Start streaming

## Apollo TV on Samsung Smart TV

Samsung uses its own Tizen OS, so you'll need a specific IPTV app.

**Method 1 — Smart IPTV App:**
1. Open the Samsung App Store
2. Search for **Smart IPTV**
3. Install and open it
4. Note your device MAC address shown in the app
5. Visit the Smart IPTV website on your PC/phone to register and add your M3U playlist
6. Restart the app on your TV

**Method 2 — Use a Firestick:**
If your Samsung TV is older, plug in an Amazon Firestick and follow the [Apollo TV Firestick guide](/blog/apollo-tv-firestick) for the easiest setup.

For a dedicated Samsung guide, see [Apollo TV on Samsung TV](/blog/apollo-tv-on-samsung-tv).

## Apollo TV on LG Smart TV

LG TVs run WebOS. Here's how to set up Apollo TV:

1. Open the **LG Content Store**
2. Search for **IPTV Smarters** or **SS IPTV**
3. Install the app
4. Open it and select **Login with Xtream Codes**
5. Enter your Apollo TV username, password, and portal URL
6. Tap **Add** and wait for everything to load

For a full LG-specific guide, check out [Apollo TV on LG TV](/blog/apollo-tv-on-lg-tv).

## Troubleshooting Apollo TV on Smart TV

**App not available in my TV's store?**
Use a Firestick, Chromecast, or Android TV box plugged into your Smart TV's HDMI port. This gives you full access to Apollo TV.

**Buffering on HD channels?**
Test your internet speed. HD requires 10 Mbps and 4K requires 25 Mbps minimum.

**Channels not loading after login?**
Make sure your subscription is active. Contact Apollo TV 24/7 support on WhatsApp for quick help.

**App crashes frequently?**
Clear the app cache in your TV's settings, or uninstall and reinstall the IPTV app.
