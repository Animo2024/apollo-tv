## What Is the Apollo Group TV App?

The **Apollo Group TV app** is not a single branded application — it's the combination of your Apollo TV IPTV subscription with a compatible IPTV player app. Apollo TV provides the subscription, credentials, and content. The IPTV player app is the interface you use to watch it.

This setup gives you maximum flexibility — you can use the best available IPTV app on each device rather than being locked into one interface.

After subscribing, you receive:
- A **username** and **password**
- A **portal URL** (server address)
- Optional **M3U playlist URL**

These credentials work with any compatible IPTV player app on any device.

## How to Install Apollo Group TV on Firestick

The Firestick is the most popular device for the Apollo Group TV app. Here's the full process:

### Step 1 — Enable Unknown Sources
- Go to Firestick **Settings** → **My Fire TV** → **Developer Options**
- Enable **Install Unknown Apps**
- Enable **ADB Debugging**

### Step 2 — Install Downloader App
- Go to the Firestick home screen
- Search for **Downloader** in the search bar
- Install the free Downloader app by AFTVnews

### Step 3 — Download IPTV Smarters Pro
- Open Downloader
- Enter code **78522** or type the IPTV Smarters APK URL
- Download and install the app

### Step 4 — Log In with Apollo TV Credentials
- Open IPTV Smarters Pro
- Select **Login with Xtream Codes API**
- Enter your Apollo TV username, password, and portal URL
- Tap **Add User**

### Step 5 — Start Watching
Your full Apollo Group TV channel lineup loads automatically. Browse Live TV, Movies, Series, and catch-up TV.

## How to Install on Android

Getting the Apollo Group TV app running on Android takes under 5 minutes:

1. Open **Google Play Store** on your Android device
2. Search for **IPTV Smarters Pro** or **TiviMate**
3. Install the app (free)
4. Open the app and select **Xtream Codes API** login
5. Enter your Apollo TV username, password, and portal URL
6. Tap **Add** — channels load automatically

**For TiviMate:** After installing, go to Add Playlist → Xtream Codes → enter your credentials. TiviMate offers a superior EPG and cleaner interface compared to IPTV Smarters.

## How to Install on iPhone and iPad

Apple devices require a slightly different approach:

1. Open the **App Store**
2. Search for **GSE Smart IPTV** or **IPTV Smarters**
3. Install the app (free)
4. Open the app → go to **Remote Playlists** or **Xtream Codes**
5. Enter your Apollo TV credentials
6. Tap **Add** — your channels appear immediately

**Tip:** GSE Smart IPTV is the most stable and fully-featured option for iOS. It handles large channel lists well and has a clean, iPad-optimized interface.

## How to Log In and Configure the App

Regardless of which device or app you use, the login process is the same:

### Xtream Codes API Login (Recommended)
This is the best login method — it loads channels, EPG, and VOD automatically:

- **Username:** provided in your Apollo TV welcome email
- **Password:** provided in your Apollo TV welcome email
- **Server URL / Portal URL:** provided in your welcome email (e.g., http://yourserver.com:8080)

### M3U Playlist Login (Alternative)
If your app supports M3U playlists:

- Copy your **M3U URL** from your welcome email
- In the app, go to Add Playlist → M3U URL
- Paste the URL and tap Add

**Important:** Never share your credentials publicly. Each subscription supports a limited number of simultaneous connections depending on your plan.

## Troubleshooting the App

### App Crashes on Launch
- Update the app to the latest version
- Clear app cache in device settings
- Reinstall the app
- Contact Apollo TV support on WhatsApp

### Channels Not Loading After Login
- Double-check your portal URL for typos
- Try logging in with the M3U URL instead of Xtream Codes
- Verify your subscription is active
- Restart the app

### No EPG / Program Guide
- Wait 5–10 minutes after first login for EPG to download
- Go to app settings and manually force an EPG refresh
- Make sure EPG is enabled in app settings

### Some Channels Missing
- Refresh your playlist in app settings
- Log out and log back in
- Contact support if specific channels are persistently missing
