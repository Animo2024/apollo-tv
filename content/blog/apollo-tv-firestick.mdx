## Step-by-Step: How to Install Apollo TV on Firestick

### Step 1 — Enable Apps from Unknown Sources

By default, Firestick blocks apps that aren't from the Amazon store. You need to allow them first:

1. Go to **Settings** on your Firestick home screen
2. Click **My Fire TV**
3. Select **Developer Options**
4. Turn on **Apps from Unknown Sources**
5. Confirm when prompted

### Step 2 — Install the Downloader App

The Downloader app is a free tool that lets you install any APK file on your Firestick.

1. Go back to the Firestick home screen
2. Use the search bar and type **"Downloader"**
3. Select the Downloader app by AFTVnews
4. Click **Download** and then **Open**
5. Allow storage access when prompted

### Step 3 — Download IPTV Smarters Pro

IPTV Smarters Pro is the recommended app for Apollo TV. It's smooth, fast, and supports all stream types.

1. Open the Downloader app
2. In the URL bar, type: `https://www.iptvsmarters.com/smarters.apk`
3. Press **Go** and wait for the download (about 1–2 minutes)
4. When prompted, click **Install**
5. After installation, click **Done** — not Open

### Step 4 — Log In With Your Apollo TV Credentials

1. Open IPTV Smarters Pro from your apps list
2. Select **Login with Xtream Codes API**
3. Enter the details from your Apollo TV welcome email:
   - **Username**
   - **Password**
   - **Portal URL**
4. Tap **Add User**
5. Wait for channels and VODs to load (this takes 1–2 minutes the first time)

### Step 5 — Start Streaming

That's it. Your Apollo TV channels, EPG guide, and VOD library are now fully loaded. Browse by category — Live TV, Movies, Series — and enjoy.

## Apollo TV Firestick Troubleshooting

**Streams not loading?**
Make sure your subscription is active and your credentials are entered correctly. Try restarting the app.

**Buffering on HD channels?**
Test your internet speed. For HD you need 10 Mbps minimum, for 4K you need 25 Mbps.

**App not installing?**
Double-check that Apps from Unknown Sources is enabled in Developer Options.

**Channels not showing?**
Force close IPTV Smarters and reopen. If the issue continues, contact Apollo TV support on WhatsApp.
