## What Does "Apollo TV Online" Mean?

Apollo TV is an IPTV service — Internet Protocol Television. This means all your TV content is delivered over the internet rather than through a cable or satellite connection.

The result? You can watch from any internet-connected device, from anywhere in the world, with no need for a satellite dish or cable box.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I watch Apollo TV online without an app?**
Some IPTV services offer web players, but using a dedicated app like IPTV Smarters gives you the best experience with EPG, catch-up TV, and category browsing.

**Does Apollo TV online work in all countries?**
Yes. Apollo TV works globally. As long as you have a reliable internet connection, you can stream from any country in the world.

**How much internet speed do I need for Apollo TV online?**
For HD streaming: 10 Mbps. For Full HD: 15–20 Mbps. For 4K: 25–30 Mbps. Most home broadband connections easily meet these requirements.

**Can I watch Apollo TV online on two devices at the same time?**
Yes. The 3-month plan supports 2 devices, the 6-month plan supports 3 devices, and the 12-month plan supports 4 devices simultaneously.

**Is Apollo TV online legal?**
IPTV technology is legal. Apollo TV provides access to licensed content. We recommend using a VPN for added privacy when streaming online.

**What happens if my internet goes down while watching?**
The stream will pause or buffer. Once your connection is restored, playback resumes automatically. Apollo TV's anti-freeze technology helps maintain streams even on slightly unstable connections.

**Can I try Apollo TV online before buying?**
Yes. Apollo TV offers a free trial. Contact the support team on WhatsApp to request your free test before committing to a subscription.
