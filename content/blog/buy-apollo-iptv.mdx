## How to Activate Your Apollo IPTV Subscription

Once you have your credentials, activating Apollo IPTV on your device takes under 10 minutes:

### On Firestick
1. Install IPTV Smarters Pro via the Downloader app
2. Open the app → select Xtream Codes API login
3. Enter your username, password, and portal URL
4. Tap Add User — channels load automatically

For the full Firestick walkthrough, visit our [Apollo TV Firestick installation guide](/blog/apollo-tv-firestick).

### On Android
1. Download IPTV Smarters Pro or TiviMate from Google Play
2. Select Xtream Codes API login
3. Enter your Apollo TV credentials
4. Tap Add — your channels appear instantly

### On iPhone/iPad
1. Download GSE Smart IPTV or IPTV Smarters from the App Store
2. Go to Remote Playlists or Xtream Codes
3. Enter your credentials and tap Add

### On Smart TV (Samsung/LG)
1. Open the Smart TV app store
2. Install IPTV Smarters or SS IPTV
3. Enter your credentials via Xtream Codes API

### On PC (Windows/Mac)
1. Download IPTV Smarters from the Microsoft Store, or use VLC
2. Enter your credentials or M3U playlist URL
3. Start watching immediately
