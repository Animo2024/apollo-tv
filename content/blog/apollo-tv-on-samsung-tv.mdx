## Method 1 — Install Smart IPTV on Samsung TV (Recommended)

Smart IPTV is the most reliable IPTV app available directly from the Samsung app store.

### Step 1 — Download Smart IPTV
1. Press the **Home** button on your Samsung remote
2. Navigate to **Apps** and open the Samsung App Store
3. Search for **Smart IPTV**
4. Install the app and open it

### Step 2 — Note Your MAC Address
When Smart IPTV opens, it shows a **MAC address** on screen. Write this down — you'll need it in the next step.

### Step 3 — Activate with Your Apollo TV Playlist
1. On your phone or PC, open a browser
2. Go to the Smart IPTV website (siptv.eu)
3. Enter your device MAC address
4. Add your Apollo TV M3U URL (provided in your Apollo TV welcome email)
5. Save the settings

### Step 4 — Reload the App
1. On your Samsung TV, close and reopen Smart IPTV
2. All your Apollo TV channels will now load automatically
3. Use the EPG to see what's on and browse by category

## Method 2 — Use IPTV Smarters on Samsung TV

Some newer Samsung TV models support IPTV Smarters directly:

1. Open the Samsung App Store
2. Search for **IPTV Smarters**
3. If available, install and open it
4. Select **Login with Xtream Codes API**
5. Enter your Apollo TV username, password, and portal URL
6. Tap **Add User** and start watching

---

## Method 3 — Use a Firestick on Samsung TV (Works on Any Model)

If your Samsung TV doesn't support IPTV apps directly, plug in an Amazon Firestick:

1. Insert the Firestick into your Samsung TV's HDMI port
2. Follow the [Apollo TV Firestick guide](/blog/apollo-tv-firestick)
3. Switch your TV input to the Firestick HDMI port
4. Enjoy Apollo TV with full 4K support

This method works on any Samsung TV with an HDMI port, regardless of the model year.

## Apollo TV Samsung TV — Troubleshooting

**Smart IPTV not showing channels?**
Make sure your M3U URL is correct and your subscription is active. Re-enter the URL in the Smart IPTV web portal.

**Buffering on 4K channels?**
You need at least 25 Mbps. Switch to a wired connection or move your router closer to the TV.

**App not available in Samsung App Store?**
Use Method 3 (Firestick) as a reliable alternative on any Samsung TV.

**Channels loading slowly?**
Clear the app cache in Settings > Apps > Smart IPTV > Clear Cache.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I watch Apollo TV on a Samsung TV without a Firestick?**
Yes. Use Smart IPTV or IPTV Smarters directly from the Samsung App Store. No Firestick is needed on newer Samsung models.

**Does Apollo TV support 4K on Samsung TV?**
Yes. Apollo TV streams 4K content on any 4K Samsung TV with a fast enough internet connection (25 Mbps minimum).

**What Samsung TV models support IPTV apps?**
Samsung Smart TVs from 2016 onwards support third-party IPTV apps via the Tizen App Store. Older models can use a Firestick.

**How many Samsung TVs can I use with one Apollo TV subscription?**
Each plan supports a set number of devices: 1 Month = 1 device, 3 Months = 2, 6 Months = 3, 12 Months = 4. You can use different devices across the allowance.

**Is there a free trial for Apollo TV on Samsung TV?**

**What's the best IPTV app for Samsung TV?**
Smart IPTV is the most reliable option specifically for Samsung Tizen OS. IPTV Smarters is also excellent if available in your region.

**Can I use Apollo TV on Samsung TV and phone at the same time?**
Yes, as long as your plan allows multiple connections. The 3-month plan and above support 2 or more simultaneous streams.
