Setting up IPTV on your **Amazon FireStick** is easier than you think. In this guide, we'll walk you through every step to get Apollo TV IPTV running on your FireStick in just a few minutes.

## What You'll Need

- Amazon FireStick (any generation)
- Apollo TV IPTV subscription credentials
- Active internet connection (10 Mbps minimum)

## Step 1: Enable Apps from Unknown Sources

Before installing IPTV Smarters, you need to allow third-party apps:

1. Go to **Settings** on your FireStick home screen
2. Select **My Fire TV** → **Developer Options**
3. Turn on **Apps from Unknown Sources**

## Step 2: Install the Downloader App

1. From the FireStick home screen, search for **"Downloader"**
2. Install the official Downloader app by AFTVnews
3. Open Downloader and allow storage permissions

## Step 3: Install IPTV Smarters Pro

1. Open Downloader and enter this URL in the address bar:
   `https://www.iptvsmarters.com/smarters.apk`
2. Click **Go** and wait for the download to complete
3. Click **Install** when prompted
4. Once installed, click **Done** (not Open)

## Step 4: Log In to Apollo TV

1. Open **IPTV Smarters Pro** from your apps
2. Select **Login with Xtream Codes API**
3. Enter your Apollo TV subscription details:
   - **Username:** (from your welcome email)
   - **Password:** (from your welcome email)
   - **URL:** (from your welcome email)
4. Click **Add User** and wait for channels to load

## Step 5: Start Watching

That's it! Your channels, VODs, and EPG will now load. Browse by category or use the search to find your favourite content.

## Troubleshooting Tips

**Buffering issues?** Check your internet speed — you need at least 10 Mbps for HD and 25 Mbps for 4K.

**Channels not loading?** Make sure your subscription is active and your credentials are entered correctly.
