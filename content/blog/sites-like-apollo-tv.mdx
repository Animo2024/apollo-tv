## Top IPTV Services Like Apollo TV in 2026

### 1. Helix TV

Helix TV is a well-known IPTV provider offering around 18,000 live channels. It has a decent VOD library and works on most devices. However, it's more expensive than Apollo TV and has fewer channels overall.

**Pros:** Clean interface, decent channel count
**Cons:** Higher price, no 4K on most plans, limited support

### 2. IPTV Smarters (as a service)

IPTV Smarters is primarily a player app, but some resellers bundle it as a full IPTV service. Quality varies depending on the reseller you choose.

**Pros:** Familiar interface, wide availability
**Cons:** Inconsistent quality, no unified support

### 3. Beast TV

Beast TV was popular a few years back with around 9,000 channels. It has faced several outages and reliability issues in recent years.

**Pros:** Affordable pricing
**Cons:** Reliability issues, limited VOD, inconsistent streams

### 4. Xtreme HD IPTV

Xtreme HD is another option in the market offering HD and 4K streams. Channel count is around 20,000. Support can be slow.

**Pros:** Good picture quality on premium channels
**Cons:** Smaller channel selection, slower support

### 5. Apollo TV IPTV (Recommended)

Apollo TV is the service this site is built on — and for good reason. It outperforms all the alternatives listed above in almost every category.

**Pros:** 40,000+ channels, 100,000+ VODs, 4K quality, anti-freeze tech, 24/7 support, best value
**Cons:** None significant
