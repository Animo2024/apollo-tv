## What Is IPTV and How Does It Work in the UK?

**IPTV** (Internet Protocol Television) delivers TV channels over the internet instead of through a satellite dish or cable connection. In the UK, this means you can watch all your favourite channels — live or on demand — through any internet-connected device.

You subscribe to an IPTV service, receive login credentials, install a free IPTV app on your device, and start watching. No engineer visits, no dish installation, no cable box rental.
