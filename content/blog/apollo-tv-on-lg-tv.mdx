## Method 1 — Install IPTV Smarters on LG TV

IPTV Smarters is available on many LG TV models and is the easiest way to use Apollo TV.

1. Press the **Home** button on your LG remote
2. Open the **LG Content Store**
3. Search for **IPTV Smarters**
4. Install and open the app
5. Select **Login with Xtream Codes API**
6. Enter your Apollo TV details:
   - Username
   - Password
   - Portal URL
7. Tap **Add User** and wait for channels to load
8. Browse and enjoy your 40,000+ channels

---

## Method 2 — Install SS IPTV on LG TV

SS IPTV is another excellent option available in the LG Content Store.

1. Open the **LG Content Store**
2. Search for **SS IPTV**
3. Install and open the app
4. Go to **Settings** inside the app
5. Select **Playlists** and add your Apollo TV M3U URL
6. Save and restart the app
7. Your channels will appear organized by category

---

## Method 3 — Use a Firestick (Works on Any LG TV)

For older LG TVs or if app store options are limited:

1. Plug an Amazon Firestick into your LG TV's HDMI port
2. Follow the complete [Apollo TV Firestick guide](/blog/apollo-tv-firestick)
3. Switch your LG TV input to the Firestick HDMI
4. Enjoy Apollo TV with full 4K support

## Apollo TV LG TV — Picture and Audio Settings

For the best viewing experience on your LG TV:

- **Picture Mode:** Use "Cinema" or "ISF Expert" for accurate color
- **TruMotion:** Turn off or set to "Clear" to avoid soap opera effect
- **HDR:** Enable if your LG TV supports HDR or Dolby Vision

## Troubleshooting Apollo TV on LG TV

**IPTV app not in LG Content Store?**
Try SS IPTV as an alternative, or use a Firestick plugged into your HDMI port.

**Channels not loading?**
Verify your Apollo TV subscription is active and your credentials are entered correctly.

**Buffering on HD channels?**
Run a speed test. HD needs 10 Mbps, 4K needs 25 Mbps. Switch to a wired Ethernet connection if possible.

**App crashing or freezing?**
Clear the app cache: Settings > App Management > Select App > Clear Cache. Then restart.

**Audio and video out of sync?**
This is usually a network issue. Restart your router and try again. If it continues, switch to a lower stream quality temporarily.
