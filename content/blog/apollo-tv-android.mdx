## How to Install Apollo TV on Android Phone or Tablet

Getting Apollo TV running on an Android phone or tablet takes under 5 minutes:

### Step 1 — Download IPTV Smarters Pro
Open the **Google Play Store** on your Android device. Search for **IPTV Smarters Pro** and tap Install. The app is free.

### Step 2 — Open the App
Once installed, open IPTV Smarters Pro from your app drawer.

### Step 3 — Select Login Method
On the home screen, tap **Login with Xtream Codes API**. This is the recommended method for Apollo TV.

### Step 4 — Enter Your Apollo TV Credentials
Fill in the three fields:
- **Username** — from your Apollo TV welcome email
- **Password** — from your Apollo TV welcome email
- **URL** — your Apollo TV portal URL (e.g., `http://yourserver.com:8080`)

Tap **Add User**.

### Step 5 — Wait for Channels to Load
Your full Apollo TV channel list loads automatically — live TV, movies, series, and catch-up content. This takes 30–60 seconds on first login.

### Step 6 — Browse and Watch
Navigate to Live TV to browse all channels. Use the search function to find specific channels quickly. Mark your favourites for instant access.

## How to Configure EPG on Android

The EPG (Electronic Program Guide) shows you what's currently playing and what's coming up — like a TV guide built into your app.

### In TiviMate
- EPG loads automatically from your Apollo TV credentials
- Go to **Settings → EPG** to set the update frequency
- Set EPG refresh to every 24 hours for accurate schedules
- EPG typically populates within 5 minutes of first login

### In IPTV Smarters
- Go to **Settings → EPG Settings**
- Enable automatic EPG loading
- Tap **Reload EPG** to force an immediate refresh

### Tips for EPG Accuracy
- Always use Xtream Codes API login (not M3U) for best EPG support
- Wait 5–10 minutes after first login before judging EPG quality
- If EPG shows incorrect times, check your device timezone settings

## Troubleshooting Apollo TV on Android

### App Crashes on Launch
- Update the app to the latest version in Google Play
- Clear app cache: Settings → Apps → IPTV Smarters → Clear Cache
- Restart your Android device
- Reinstall the app if the issue persists

### Wrong Credentials Error
- Double-check for typos — credentials are case-sensitive
- Make sure there are no extra spaces before or after the text
- Verify your portal URL is entered exactly as provided
- Contact Apollo TV WhatsApp support if credentials still don't work

### Buffering on Android
- Switch from Wi-Fi to Ethernet (via USB adapter for phones/tablets)
- Increase buffer size in app settings to 10 seconds
- Lower stream quality from 4K to FHD temporarily
- Close all background apps to free up RAM
- Restart your router

### No Channels Loading After Login
- Pull to refresh your playlist
- Log out and log back in
- Try M3U login instead of Xtream Codes
- Check if your subscription is still active

### EPG Not Showing
- Wait 10 minutes after first login
- Go to app settings and force an EPG refresh
- Make sure EPG is enabled in settings
