## Does Apollo Group TV Have an Official Roku App?

## How to Set Up Apollo Group TV on Roku

Follow these steps to get **Apollo Group TV on Roku** running:

### Step 1 — Add the IPTV App to Roku
1. Press the **Home** button on your Roku remote
2. Go to **Streaming Channels** → **Search Channels**
3. Search for **IPTV Smarters** or **OTT Navigator**
4. Select the app and click **Add Channel**
5. Wait for installation to complete

### Step 2 — Open the App
Once installed, go back to the Roku home screen and open the newly installed IPTV app from your channel list.

### Step 3 — Log In with Apollo TV Credentials
In the app, select **Add Playlist** or **Xtream Codes API** login:
- **Username** — from your Apollo TV welcome email
- **Password** — from your Apollo TV welcome email
- **Server URL** — your Apollo TV portal URL

Tap **Connect** or **Add** and wait for your channels to load.

### Step 4 — Browse and Watch
Your full Apollo Group TV channel lineup will appear — organized by category. Browse live TV, movies, series, and sports. The EPG (electronic program guide) loads automatically.

### Step 5 — Optimize Settings
Go into the app settings and increase the buffer size to 5–10 seconds for smoother live streams. Enable hardware decoding if the option is available.

## Troubleshooting Common Roku Issues

### Channels Not Loading
- Double-check your username, password, and portal URL for typos
- Confirm your Apollo TV subscription is active
- Restart the Roku device and reopen the app
- Contact Apollo TV support on WhatsApp for instant help

### Buffering on Roku
- Switch from Wi-Fi to a wired Ethernet connection (Roku supports Ethernet via adapter)
- Lower stream quality from 4K to Full HD temporarily
- Increase buffer size in app settings
- Restart your router

### App Not Found in Roku Store
- Make sure your Roku is updated to the latest firmware
- Try searching with different spelling (e.g., "IPTV Smarters Pro")
- Use the Roku web portal (my.roku.com) to add channels remotely

### EPG Not Displaying
- Wait 5–10 minutes for EPG data to load after first login
- Go to app settings and manually refresh the EPG
- Verify your portal URL is entered correctly

## Tips for the Best Streaming Experience on Roku

- **Connect via Ethernet** — Roku supports wired connections via USB Ethernet adapter. Wired is always more stable than Wi-Fi for live sports.
- **Close other apps** — Roku devices have limited RAM. Close all other channels before opening your IPTV app.
- **Keep Roku updated** — outdated firmware can cause compatibility issues with IPTV apps.
- **Use 5GHz Wi-Fi** — if you can't use Ethernet, the 5GHz band is faster and less congested than 2.4GHz.
- **Reboot weekly** — a simple weekly restart keeps Roku running smoothly.
- **Test before big events** — always verify streams are working a few hours before a major game.
