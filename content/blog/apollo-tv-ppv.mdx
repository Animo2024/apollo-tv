## Tips for the Best PPV Streaming Experience

PPV events are high-demand viewing moments. Follow these tips to make sure nothing goes wrong on fight night:

- **Test your setup 24 hours before** — don't wait until the prelims start to test your stream
- **Use Ethernet** — plug your streaming device directly into your router for maximum stability during peak demand
- **Check internet speed** — run a test at fast.com. You need 25+ Mbps for 4K PPV streams
- **Have a backup stream ready** — Apollo TV usually provides multiple sources per channel. Know where the backup stream is before you need it
- **Restart your router and device** — do this 30 minutes before the event to clear any cached issues
- **Increase buffer size** — set to 10 seconds in your IPTV app settings
- **Close all other apps** — free up device RAM before the event starts
- **Contact support early** — if you have any issues, WhatsApp support responds in under 5 minutes. Don't wait until the main event
