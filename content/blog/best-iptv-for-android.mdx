## IPTV Android Tips for Best Performance

Follow these tips to get the most out of IPTV on Android:

- **Use Ethernet on Android TV boxes** — plug in via USB Ethernet adapter for stable live sports
- **Enable hardware decoding** — in app settings, enable hardware acceleration for smoother 4K playback
- **Set buffer to 10 seconds** — prevents freezing during live events
- **Close background apps** — free up RAM before watching IPTV on lower-end Android devices
- **Use 5GHz Wi-Fi** — faster and less congested than 2.4GHz
- **Keep apps updated** — regular updates fix compatibility issues and improve stability
- **Use TiviMate for TV boxes** — its interface is specifically designed for remote control navigation
- **Enable picture-in-picture** — watch IPTV while browsing on Android phones
